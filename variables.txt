A quick overview of the data variables 

Sepal Length 
Measures the length of the sepal, which is each of the parts of the calyx of a flower, enclosing the petals and typically green and leaflike 

Sepal Width 
Measures the width of the sepal

Petal Length 
The length of the petal, the petals are modified leaves that surround the reproductive parts of flowers 

Petal width 
The width of the petals 

Species 
Shows which of the 3 iris species that the current data points to
